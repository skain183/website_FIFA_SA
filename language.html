🗣️ Must-Know Phrases for Visitors to Saudi Arabia

  • Marhaban (مرحبا): Hello
  • Shukran (شكراً): Thank you
  • Min fadlik (من فضلك): Please
  • Kam al-thaman? (كم الثمن؟): How much is it?
  • Ayna al-hammam? (أين الحمام؟): Where is the bathroom?
  • La afham (لا أفهم): I don’t understand
  • Naam / Laa (نعم / لا): Yes / No
  • Hal tatahaddath al-Ingleeziyah? (هل تتحدث الإنجليزية؟): Do you speak English?
